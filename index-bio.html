Hailing from Brooklyn, New York, Parquet Courts make scathing new age indie punk when we need it most. Preceeded by the artwork of chief lyricist Andrew Savage, the band attempts to subvert the absurdist nature of life in general, without coming across as preachy. Their early work is raw and punchy, their later produce infused with funk and soul. But regardless of the release, you can bet your bottom dollar the wit will be dry and the message pertinent.
